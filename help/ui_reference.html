Accounts Reconciliation Terms and Calculations

Checking Reconciliation

TermCaptured or ComputedDefinition / Formula
Beginning BalanceCapturedUser-entered month opening register balance.
Statement EndingCapturedEnding balance from bank statement.
Statement DateCapturedDate of statement ending value.
Pending DepositsComputedSum of uncleared positive transactions.
Pending WithdrawalsComputedSum of absolute value of uncleared negative transactions.
Net PendingComputedPending Deposits - Pending Withdrawals.
Cleared Register BalanceComputedRunning Ending Balance - Net Pending.
Adjusted Statement BalanceComputedStatement Ending + Net Pending.
DifferenceComputedAdjusted Statement Balance - Running Ending Balance.
StatusComputedBalanced when Difference = 0; otherwise Out of balance.

Credit Reconciliation

TermCaptured or ComputedDefinition / Formula
Line of CreditCaptured (Settings > Accounts)Credit limit for the account.
Statement BalanceCapturedDebt amount from credit statement (entered as positive amount).
Statement DateCapturedStatement cutoff date used to evaluate cleared post-statement activity.
Reported Current BalanceCapturedCurrent debt shown by issuer (stored for reference).
Reported Available CreditCapturedAvailable credit shown by issuer.
Computed Current BalanceComputedStatement Balance + debt delta from cleared transactions after Statement Date.
Computed Available CreditComputedLine of Credit - Computed Current Balance.
DifferenceComputedReported Available Credit - Computed Available Credit.
StatusComputedReconciled when Difference = 0, otherwise Needs Review. Additional states: missing line of credit / missing reported available credit.

For credit accounts, checking-only metrics are intentionally hidden in the UI.
